# Server Features

## World Generation
- **Terralith** — overhauled terrain generation with 100+ new biomes to explore
- **Better End** — massive overhaul of the End dimension with new biomes and structures
- **Better Nether** — overhauls the Nether with new biomes, plants, and mobs
- **Multiworld** — multiple world support

## Exploration & Structures
- **YUNG's Better Dungeons** — completely overhauled dungeons with new layouts and challenges
- **YUNG's Better Nether Fortresses** — redesigned Nether Fortresses that are larger and more dangerous
- **YUNG's Better End Island** — a more impressive and varied End island to conquer
- **Structory** — new handcrafted structures scattered across the world to discover
- **True Ending: Ender Dragon Overhaul** — makes the Ender Dragon fight more dynamic and challenging

## Create & Engineering
- **Create** — build automated factories and contraptions using rotating shafts, gears, and conveyors
- **Create: Steam 'n' Rails** — adds trains, railways, and steam-powered transport
- **Create Crafts & Additions** — adds electric motors, generators, and more Create-compatible machines
- **Create: Escalated** — adds escalators and moving walkways

## Space Exploration
- **Ad Astra** — build rockets and travel to other planets and moons
- **Planets+** — adds even more celestial bodies to visit

## Transportation
- **Immersive Aircraft** — craftable planes and airships you can fly
- **Zipline** — shoot zipline hooks between points and ride them across gaps
- **MTR (Minecraft Transit Railway)** — build your own subway and train network with working stations
- **MTR Station Decoration Addon** — extra decoration blocks for building realistic transit stations
- **Pyrotechnic Elytra** — firework-powered elytra boosting

## Combat & Survival
- **Void Totem** — a totem that saves you from falling into the void
- **Charm of Undying** — a reusable charm that acts like a totem of undying
- **Tool Leveling+** — tools gain experience and level up as you use them
- **More Armor Trims** — additional armor trim patterns to customize your gear

## Inventory & Storage
- **Sophisticated Backpacks** — feature-rich backpacks with upgrade slots for sorting, filtering, and auto-pickup
- **Carry On** — pick up and carry storage blocks, machines, and even mobs with your bare hands
- **Dimensional Inventories** — access your inventory across dimensions

## Food & Farming
- **Farmer's Delight** — overhauls cooking and farming with new crops, meals, and kitchen tools
- **AlcoCraft+** — adds alcoholic drinks and brewing mechanics
- **Fabric Seasons** — adds seasons that affect crop growth, weather, and the environment

## Furniture & Decoration
- **Handcrafted** — craftable furniture including sofas, tables, shelves, and more
- **Macaw's Bridges** — decorative bridges, handrails, and walkways in many styles
- **Amendments** — extra decorative functionality added to vanilla blocks
- **Supplementaries** — decorative items like rope, lanterns, sign posts, and display cases
- **Fairy Lights** — string up customizable fairy light decorations anywhere
- **Connectible Chains** — hang chains between fences and posts decoratively
- **Bits and Chisels** — chisel individual pixels out of blocks to create detailed custom shapes

## Cosmetics & Avatars
- **Figura** — create and wear fully custom 3D avatars
- **Simple Hats** — wear cosmetic hats on top of your normal helmet
- **Accessories / Trinkets** — equip rings and baubles in dedicated slots for extra effects
- **Elytra Slot** — wear an elytra without sacrificing your chestplate
- **Estrogen** — additional cosmetic and customization options
- **Chat Heads** — shows a player's head icon next to their chat messages

## Mobs
- **Copper Golem** — brings back the copper golem from the 2021 mob vote
- **Ribbits** — new frog variants and amphibian content

## Utility & QoL
- **Jade** — shows detailed info about whatever block or entity you're looking at
- **Just Enough Items (JEI)** — browse all crafting recipes and item uses in-game
- **Xaero's World Map** — a full world map with waypoints
- **WorldEdit** — powerful in-game terrain editing tools
- **Replay Mod** — record and replay gameplay sessions from any camera angle
- **Simple Voice Chat** — proximity voice chat; the closer a player is, the louder they sound
- **Waystones** — teleport to set locations around the world
- **Ledger** — logs block changes and interactions for admin oversight
- **Discord MC Chat** — links in-game chat with Discord
- **Audio Player** — play custom audio in-game via music discs and items
- **Camerapture** — a craftable in-game camera for taking photos
- **Clumps** — groups nearby XP orbs together for cleaner pickup
- **Simple Copper Pipes** — fluid transport using copper pipes

## Misc
- **Musical Instruments** — playable instruments like guitars and flutes
- **Spud's Shops** — player-run shops for trading items
- **Joy of Painting** — paint custom paintings and hang them on walls
- **Landmines** — placeable landmines for traps and defense
